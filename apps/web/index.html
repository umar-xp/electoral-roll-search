Quick Guide

📌 Note
Few districts are added in the full search. For other districts (not available in the dropdown), use the following 3 steps.
1
Visit voters.eci.gov.in and look for the most likely polling station or part number in which your name may exist — there may be a few possible PDFs.
2
Go to ceo.karnataka.gov.in/voter_list/en and download the PDF(s) for your AC and part number. File names look like A0850001.pdf — do not rename them.
3
Upload the PDF(s) below and enter your name and father's / husband's name in the search box. The tool will OCR and search the roll for you.
